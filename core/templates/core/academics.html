Our Teaching Approach

Student-Centered Learning

We focus on active participation, critical thinking, and student engagement rather than passive learning.

Project-Based Learning

Students learn through hands-on projects that develop problem-solving skills and practical application of knowledge.

Technology Integration

We incorporate modern technologies into our teaching methods to prepare students for the digital world.

Collaborative Learning

Students work in teams to solve problems, complete projects, and learn from each other's perspectives.

Science Laboratories

Computer Laboratory

Smart Classrooms

University Preparation

At Glad Tidings School, we take pride in preparing our students for successful university applications and higher education. Our comprehensive university preparation program includes:

  • Guidance on university selection and application procedures
  • Assistance with personal statements and application essays
  • Preparation for university entrance examinations (UTME, SAT, IELTS, TOEFL)
  • Career counseling and course selection advice
  • University visits and higher education fairs
  • Alumni networking and mentorship opportunities
