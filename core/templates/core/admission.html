Our Admission Process

1
Application Submission

Complete and submit the online application form along with the required documents and application fee.

2
Document Verification

Our admissions team will review your application and verify all submitted documents.

3
Entrance Assessment

Successful applicants will be invited to take an entrance assessment to evaluate academic readiness.

4
Student & Parent Interview

A personal interview will be conducted with the student and parents to understand expectations and ensure a good fit.

5
Admission Decision

Based on all evaluations, the admission committee will make a decision and notify applicants.

6
Enrollment & Registration

Accepted students will complete the enrollment process by paying the required fees and submitting any additional documents.

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate (original and copy)
  • Academic records from previous school
  • Passport photographs (4 recent)
  • Transfer certificate (if applicable)
  • Immunization records
  • Parent/Guardian ID
  • Proof of address

Scholarships

Glad Tidings School offers various scholarship opportunities to recognize and support outstanding students:

Academic Excellence Scholarship

Awarded to students with exceptional academic performance. Covers up to 50% of tuition fees.

Sports Scholarship

Available to students who demonstrate extraordinary talent and achievement in sports. Covers up to 30% of tuition fees.

Arts & Culture Scholarship

Awarded to students with exceptional abilities in music, visual arts, or performing arts. Covers up to 30% of tuition fees.

Financial Need-Based Scholarship

Available to deserving students with demonstrated financial need. Coverage varies based on assessment.

Scholarship applications require additional documentation. Please download the scholarship application form from our website or request it from the Admissions Office.
