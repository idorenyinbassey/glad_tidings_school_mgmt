Step by Step to Academic Excellence

Our History

Glad Tidings College Mbiabong Eto Uyo Was Established In September 2001 With An Initial Intake Of Thirty Eight (38) Students And A Staff Strenght Of Five(5).

The Proprietor Elder Edet Effiong Vested With God�S Wisdom And Understanding Of What It Means To Establish A Secondary School, Vigorously Pursued For Approval To Open The Institution.

In 2002, The School Was Granted Approval By The Akwa Ibom State Ministry Of Education To Operate As A Private Secondary School. Since Then, Glad Tidings College Has Grown Significantly, Expanding Its Facilities, Curriculum, And Student Body.

Today, we are proud to be one of the leading educational institutions in the region, known for our commitment to academic excellence, character development, and community service.

Over the years, we have consistently maintained our commitment to fostering a learning environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally. Our graduates have gone on to excel in various fields, becoming leaders and contributors to society.

Our Campus Through the Years

From humble beginnings to a modern educational facility, our campus has evolved while maintaining its core values and commitment to excellence.

From the Principal’s Desk

Glad Tidings College is a co-educational institution that provides effective and standard learning environment that promote and encourage academic excellence, creativity and self-improvement.

Our dedicated team of teachers and staff work relentlessly to ensure that every learner receives the support and guidance they need to thrive in all the Science, Art and Commercial subjects.

We believed in motivating our students to become self-assured, optimistic, dutiful, accountable, bold, humane and skilled individuals who will make a valuable contribution and meaningful difference in the society.
